Main Types of iPhone 18 Screen Protectors

HD Clear Screen Protector

HD clear glass provides maximum display clarity, high transparency, and smooth touch experience. It remains the baseline choice for global distributors.

Privacy Screen Protector

Utilizing anti-peep technology and side-view blocking, these protectors ensure data privacy protection and occupy a premium pricing position in the market.

AR Screen Protector

Reduces reflections and improves outdoor visibility. AR technology greatly enhances screen clarity, making it a favorite for outdoor users.

Camera Lens Protector

Camera modules continue to become larger and more expensive. Our Premium Lens Protectors prevent scratches on the camera lens array while maintaining original image quality.
