Why Traditional Screen Protectors Are No Longer Enough

For years, screen protectors have been designed to solve three main problems:

  • Prevent scratches
  • Reduce impact damage
  • Resist fingerprints

Today's users also expect greater visual comfort during prolonged screen use.

Introducing Quantum Red-Light Technology

Unlike conventional screen protectors that simply filter or absorb certain wavelengths, HSONE's Red Light Screen Protector incorporates Quantum Dot Active Light Conversion Technology. Instead of blocking large amounts of visible light, the film is engineered to convert a portion of high-energy blue light into red light within the visible spectrum while maintaining excellent brightness and color performance.

Why Quantum Dot Technology Matters

Quantum dots are nano-scale semiconductor materials capable of converting specific wavelengths of light with high efficiency. HSONE uses this technology to actively convert part of blue light into red light while preserving high transparency and natural color reproduction.
