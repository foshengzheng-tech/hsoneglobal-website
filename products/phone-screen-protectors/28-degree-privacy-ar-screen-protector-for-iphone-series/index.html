28° Privacy AR Screen Protector For iPhone Series： PRIVACY AR (28° Privacy + ANTI-REFLECTION GLASS) Screen Protector This screen protector combines AR anti-reflection technology with 28° anti-peeping privacy protection. Made of CSG glass with Mohs 6 hardness (SGS certified), it has a light transmittance of ≥68% and reflectivity reduced to <0.5%. It features electroplated AF coating for enhanced anti-fingerprint and wear-resistant performance. Key Highlights • AR anti-reflection technology, reflectivity <0.5% (SGS certified) for reduced glare • 28° anti-peeping visible angle, effectively protecting screen privacy • Mohs 6 hardness (SGS certified) for excellent scratch resistance • ≥68% light transmittance, balancing privacy and screen visibility • Electroplated AF coating with >10,000 times wear resistance (SGS certified) for smooth touch and anti-fouling • Passed 64g/120cm ball drop test, ensuring impact resistance TYPE:2.5D Clear/2.5D Full Cover/3D Resin Edge/3D Curved Glass
