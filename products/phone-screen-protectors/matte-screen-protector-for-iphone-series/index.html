Matte Screen Protector For iPhone Series： MATTE GLASS (ANTI-GLARE GLASS) Screen Protector This anti-glare matte screen protector features AG anti-glare technology for an e-sports gaming feel, eliminating screen glare and shine. Made with Mohs 5 hardness glass, it offers high-definition clarity with ≥90% light transmittance, paired with magnetron sputtering coating and electroplated AF coating for balanced quality and smooth, anti-fouling performance. Key Highlights • AG anti-glare technology with matte finish, eliminating screen glare and shine • E-sports gaming feel for smooth, precise touch control • Mohs 5 hardness glass with ≥90% light transmittance, maintaining high-definition clarity • Electroplated AF coating with oleophobic and hydrophobic properties for anti-fouling and silk-like smooth touch • Magnetron sputtering coating process for balanced and consistent coating quality • Micron-polished surface for enhanced durability and touch experience TYPE:2.5D Clear/2.5D Full Cover/3D Resin Edge/3D Curved Glass/3D Hot bending Glass Functional Options: Privacy/Anti Blue light/Anti Reflective/Anti Glare/Anti-Rainbow/Red-Light
